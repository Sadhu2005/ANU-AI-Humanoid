Our Mission: A Targeted Solution

To empower students by deploying ANU 6.0: an accessible, AI-powered humanoid robot designed for distraction-free, personalized learning. It serves as a mentor, a friend, and a gateway to new technology, speaking both English and Kannada.

Meet ANU 6.0: A New Kind of Companion

üéì A Personalized Mentor & Guide

ANU 6.0 doesn't use a one-size-fits-all approach. It assesses a student's level, creates a custom learning path, and acts as a personal teacher. It provides access to new information and technology, guiding them through daily lessons in grammar and vocabulary.

ü§ù An Intelligent Friend to Practice With

As a friendly companion, ANU 6.0 removes the fear of making mistakes. Students can practice English pronunciation without hesitation. It speaks both Kannada and English, ensuring true understanding.

üöÄ An AI That Learns With You

Like modern AI assistants, ANU 6.0 learns from interactions. The more a student talks to it, the better it adapts its teaching style, becoming a more effective guide over time.

ü§ó Engaging Physical Interaction

Beyond the screen, ANU 6.0 offers physical engagement. It can gesture, wave, and even give a hug, creating a stronger bond than a digital tool. This physical presence helps maintain focus and makes learning fun.

Technology Stack

‚öôÔ∏è Hardware

  • Raspberry Pi 5: The central brain for all processing.
  • 17 DOF Servos: MG995 motors for precise and powerful humanoid movements.
  • PCA9685 Driver: Manages the 16+ servos smoothly without overloading the Pi.
  • Webcam & Mic: The robot's eyes and ears for vision and speech recognition.
  • Sensors: Ultrasonic, PIR, and Temperature sensors for environmental awareness.

üíª Software

  • Python: The core programming language for the entire system.
  • OpenCV: Powers face and object recognition capabilities.
  • SpeechRecognition Libs: For processing voice commands in both languages.
  • SQLite: An on-device database for storing knowledge, lessons, and user data.
  • Custom AI Models: Localized models for offline decision-making and interaction.
