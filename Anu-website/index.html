The Challenge We Face

🌍

The Language Barrier

In rural Karnataka, students face a critical challenge: no environment to practice English outside the classroom. This creates a vicious cycle where lack of practice leads to poor fluency, which limits future opportunities.

Impact: 73% of rural students struggle with English communication

📱

The Digital Paradox

While smartphones promise educational access, they've become the biggest distraction. Students spend 4+ hours daily on social media and games, while educational apps remain unused.

Reality: 82% of screen time is non-educational

The Cascading Effect

Economic Disparity

Introducing ANU 6.0

A revolutionary AI-powered humanoid robot that transforms education by becoming a personalized mentor, an intelligent friend, and a gateway to knowledge.

Personalized Mentor

Adapts to each student's learning pace, creating custom lesson plans and tracking progress in real-time.

Bilingual Communication

Speaks both Kannada and English, explaining complex concepts in native language for better understanding.

Physical Interaction

17 DOF allows natural gestures, creating emotional connection through waves, hugs, and expressions.

Offline-First Design

Works without internet, perfect for rural areas with limited connectivity. Syncs when online.

Adaptive AI

Learns from every interaction, continuously improving its teaching methods and responses.

Safe & Focused

Distraction-free learning environment with no games or social media. Pure educational focus.
