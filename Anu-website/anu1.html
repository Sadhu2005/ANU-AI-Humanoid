The Challenge: A Deeper Look

For rural students, learning English is more than an academic exerciseâ€”it's a gateway to future opportunities. However, several critical barriers stand in their way.

The Environment Gap

Students lack an English-speaking environment at home, making it nearly impossible to practice conversationally. This limits their fluency and confidence.

The Distraction Dilemma

While mobile phones offer resources, they are also a primary source of distraction. A physical robot provides a focused, engaging, and dedicated learning companion.

The Foundation for Future Learning

A strong foundation in English is essential for higher education in technology and innovation. By understanding the language, students can better grasp complex technical subjects.

Our Mission: A Targeted Solution

To empower students by deploying ANU 6.0: an accessible, AI-powered humanoid robot designed for distraction-free, personalized learning. It serves as a mentor, a friend, and a gateway to new technology, speaking both English and Kannada.

Meet ANU 6.0: A New Kind of Companion

Why a Physical Robot?

Unlike apps or software, ANU 6.0's physical presence creates a stronger emotional connection, reduces distractions, and enables interactive learning through gestures and movements that make education more engaging and memorable.

A Personalized Mentor & Guide

ANU 6.0 doesn't use a one-size-fits-all approach. It assesses a student's level, creates a custom learning path, and acts as a personal teacher. It provides access to new information and technology, guiding them through daily lessons in grammar and vocabulary.

An Intelligent Friend to Practice With

As a friendly companion, ANU 6.0 removes the fear of making mistakes. Students can practice English pronunciation without hesitation. It speaks both Kannada and English, explaining difficult concepts in the native language to ensure true understanding.

An AI That Learns With You

Like modern AI assistants, ANU 6.0 learns from its interactions. The more a student talks to it, the better it understands their needs and adapts its teaching style, becoming a more effective guide over time.

Engaging Physical Interaction

Beyond the screen, ANU 6.0 offers physical engagement. It can gesture, wave, and even give a hug, creating a stronger, more memorable bond than a purely digital tool. This physical presence helps maintain focus and makes learning more fun and interactive.

Offline-First Design

Specifically designed for areas with limited internet connectivity, ANU 6.0 works primarily offline, using on-device AI models. It only needs occasional internet access for updates and syncing.

Technology Stack

Hardware

  • Raspberry Pi 5: The central brain for all processing.
  • 17 DOF Servos: MG995 motors for precise and powerful humanoid movements.
  • PCA9685 Driver: Manages the 16+ servos smoothly without overloading the Pi.
  • Webcam & Mic: The robot's eyes and ears for vision and speech recognition.
  • Sensors: Ultrasonic, PIR, and Temperature sensors for environmental awareness.

Software

  • Python: The core programming language for the entire system.
  • OpenCV: Powers face and object recognition capabilities.
  • SpeechRecognition Libs: For processing voice commands in both languages.
  • SQLite: An on-device database for storing knowledge, lessons, and user data.
  • Custom AI Models: Localized models for offline decision-making and interaction.
